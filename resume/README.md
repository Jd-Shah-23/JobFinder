# Resume and Cover Letter

## Instructions

1. **Add your resume**: Place your resume PDF file here and name it `resume.pdf`
2. **Add cover letter** (optional): Create a `cover_letter.txt` file with your cover letter template

## Resume Requirements

- **Format**: PDF (recommended) or DOCX
- **File name**: `resume.pdf`
- **Size**: Keep under 2MB for best compatibility
- **Content**: Make sure it's updated with your latest experience

## Cover Letter Template (Optional)

Create a file named `cover_letter.txt` with your cover letter. You can use placeholders:

```
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the {job_title} position at {company_name}.

With {experience_years} years of experience in Java development, I have developed strong expertise in:
- Java, Spring Boot, and Microservices
- REST API development
- Database design and optimization
- Agile development methodologies

I am particularly excited about this opportunity at {company_name} because...

[Add your personalized content here]

Thank you for considering my application.

Best regards,
{your_name}
```

## Important Notes

- ⚠️ **Privacy**: This folder is included in `.gitignore` to protect your personal information
- ⚠️ **Do NOT commit** your actual resume to a public repository
- ✓ Keep your resume updated regularly
- ✓ Tailor your cover letter for better results
